Fruits

Fruits can be basically defined as the edible product of plant growth which can serve as food for humans and animals. Fruits are important because they are an excellent source of essential vitamins and minerals and they are high in fibre. Fruits are also said to provide a wide range of health-boosting antioxidants.

Some examples of the most common fruits

Eating apples is linked to a lower risk of many chronic conditions.

Eating grapes lowers blood pressure and improves bone health.

Eating oranges makes it easier to absorb iron to fight anemia.

Eating strawberries benefits the heart health.

During difficult times, it's best to cut down on sweets like cookies, cake and candy. Satisfy your sweet tooth with fruits to help prevent blood sugar dips and spikes.

-Karen Salmansohn
